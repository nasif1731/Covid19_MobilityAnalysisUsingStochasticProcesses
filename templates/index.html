Welcome!

This dashboard helps you understand how public movement changed during the COVID-19 pandemic using real-world mobility data.

🧭 Choose a Module

  • Mobility Trends: Explore how people transitioned between different levels of mobility using Markov Chains.
  • Behavior Analysis: Reveal hidden behavioral patterns using Hidden Markov Models (HMM).
  • Crowd Simulator: Simulate and understand congestion at public places using M/M/1 queuing theory.

💡 Why This Matters

Insights from mobility patterns can guide policy makers and health officials to prepare better for future emergencies and optimize public services like healthcare, transportation, and essential stores.

🌍 Data Source

Based on Google COVID-19 Community Mobility Reports.
